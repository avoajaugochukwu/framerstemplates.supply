# 10 Free and Paid Framer Portfolio Templates to Elevate Your Online Presence

In today's digital world, having a strong online portfolio is essential for designers, developers, and creatives looking to showcase their work and attract new opportunities. A well-crafted portfolio not only reflects your skills and style but also serves as a powerful marketing tool.

**Framer**, a no-code/low-code design platform, makes it easier than ever to create high-performance, visually stunning websites without writing complex code. If you're new to Framer portfolios, start with our [complete Framer portfolio guide](/blog/framer-portfolio-guide). With its seamless animations, responsive layouts, and intuitive design tools, Framer is an excellent choice for building a professional portfolio.

To help you get started, we've curated a list of 10 Framer portfolio templates—five free and five paid—that can elevate your online presence. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your current portfolio, these templates offer sleek, modern designs and powerful features to make your work stand out.

## Free Templates

### 1. Amberite

Amberite is a sleek and contemporary template designed for creatives seeking an elegant online showcase. It offers seven unique pages, including Home, About, Work, Blog, Store, Project Detail, and 404.

**Features:**

- Fully responsive
- Framer Dynamic CMS
- Built-in components and styles
- Custom effects and interactions
- Automatic light and dark modes
- Smooth scrolling

### 2. Lando

Lando is a beautifully designed portfolio template perfect for showcasing creative work. It features a clean, minimalist design with bold typography and effective use of white space to ensure your work stands out.

**Features:**

- Minimalist design with bold typography
- Clean and structured layout
- Ideal for visual artists, designers, and photographers

### 3. Bronx

Bronx is a sleek and professional portfolio template with a focus on visual appeal. It offers both light and dark modes, providing versatility for different styles of work.

**Features:**

- Light and dark mode options
- Minimalistic layout to highlight work
- Clean and modern aesthetics
- Day/Night Theme
- Animations/Effects

### 4. Pearl

Pearl is a modern portfolio template built for Framer, perfect for UI/UX professionals and creatives. It includes multiple pages such as Home, About, Work, Work Detail, 404, and Contact.

**Features:**

- Full customization
- CMS integration for easy content management
- Clean design with modern UI components
- CMS System
- Scroll effects & interactions

### 5. Oslo

Oslo is a sleek and modern creative portfolio template, ideal for designers, photographers, and artists. It offers a clean and minimal design that highlights your work without clutter.

**Features:**

- Essential pages (Home, About, Projects, Contact, 404)
- Fully customizable and free
- Optimized for performance

## Paid Templates

### 1. Luca

Luca is an elegant and modern portfolio template tailored for designers aiming to showcase their work with sophistication. It features a minimalistic design complemented by sophisticated animations and interactions.

**Features:**

- Fully customizable colors, typography, and layouts
- Dynamic CMS for simplified project management
- High performance with 100% Lighthouse score
- Smooth animations and interactions

### 2. Prisma

Prisma offers a sleek and customizable design, perfect for UI/UX professionals, freelancers, and creatives. It comes with versatile pages such as Home, Projects, Store, About Me, and 404, along with CMS pages for your projects.

**Features:**

- Versatile layout with multiple pages
- CMS integration for projects
- Professional and visually appealing aesthetic

### 3. Dashfolio+

Dashfolio+ is a premium dashboard-styled portfolio template ideal for designers, marketers, and developers. It connects to a CMS for blogs, products, stacks, and case studies, and features a beautiful sidebar navigation resembling a dashboard.

**Features:**

- Dashboard-like interface with sidebar navigation
- CMS for blogs, products, and case studies
- Membership functionality and advanced search options
- Mobile-friendly design with keyboard shortcuts

### 4. Blnk

Blnk is a minimalistic portfolio template designed for creative freelancers, UI designers, and visual designers. It offers a clean and modern design with sleek animations and an elegant layout.

**Features:**

- Multiple pages (Home, About, Portfolio, Contact, Blog, CMS Pages)
- Simple customization without coding
- Elegant and modern UI design

### 5. PaperWhite

PaperWhite is a meticulously designed personal portfolio template tailored for product designers and UX professionals. It offers a clean, vibrant, and highly customizable design to effectively showcase your work.

**Features:**

- Designed for product designers and UX professionals
- Highly customizable layout and elements
- Optimized for ease of use and accessibility
