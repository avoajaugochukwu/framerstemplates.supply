# Getting Started with Framer Templates

Framer templates are pre-built website designs that you can customize and publish without writing a single line of code. Whether you're building a portfolio, landing page, or SaaS website, there's a template that fits your needs.

## Why Use a Template?

Starting from scratch is time-consuming. Templates give you:

- **Professional design** out of the box
- **Responsive layouts** that work on every device
- **Built-in interactions** and animations
- **Faster time to launch** — hours instead of weeks

## How to Choose the Right Template

When browsing templates, consider these factors:

1. **Purpose** — Is it for a portfolio, startup, agency, or personal brand?
2. **Style** — Does the aesthetic match your brand?
3. **Pages** — Does it include all the pages you need?
4. **Customizability** — Can you easily swap colors, fonts, and content?

## Customizing Your Template

Once you've picked a template, customization is straightforward:

### Colors and Fonts

Most templates use design tokens for colors and typography. Change them once, and the entire site updates.

### Content

Replace placeholder text and images with your own. Framer's visual editor makes this drag-and-drop simple.

### Adding Pages

Need an extra page? Duplicate an existing one and modify it. The layout system keeps everything consistent.

## Publishing

When you're happy with the result, hit publish. Framer handles hosting, SSL, and performance optimization automatically.
