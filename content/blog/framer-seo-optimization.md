## Google Analytics vs. Google Search Console

Understanding the difference between these two tools is the first step to mastering SEO for your Framer site.

### Google Analytics

Google Analytics is **in-page analytics** that focuses on what happens *on* your site:

- It shows how long people spend on your site
- It provides insights into how users interact with your site
- It displays where most of your users are coming from

### Google Search Console

Google Search Console is **search-driven and outward-focused**:

- It helps with SEO optimization
- It focuses on how your site appears in search results

## First Steps in SEO Optimization

Before diving into advanced strategies, get these basics set up:

1. Set up **Google Search Console**
2. Set up **Google Analytics**
3. Conduct a simple audit using [SEOptimer.com](https://www.seoptimer.com/) (a free tool)
4. Submit your URL to get a detailed SEO report

## Running an SEO Audit with SEOptimer

SEOptimer provides a high-level SEO report that is actionable. It is better than Google Lighthouse for SEO analysis.

The report is divided into several categories:

- **Usability**
- **Performance**
- **Social**
- **On-page SEO**
- **Links**
- **Other factors**

## Prioritizing SEO Improvements

### 1. Link Building (Highest Priority)

Backlinks are crucial for ranking well in search results. You need backlinks from **high-domain authority websites**.

Use [Ahrefs.com](https://ahrefs.com/) to check domain authority:

- Domain authority ranges from **0 to 100**
- New websites have **0** domain authority
- Strong sites (CNN, Forbes, etc.) have **92+** domain authority

Link building is a major strategy that can significantly improve SEO.

### 2. Meta Descriptions

Meta descriptions improve click-through rates. Framer provides a way to update meta descriptions — check Framer's documentation for the exact steps.

### 3. Header Tags

Use **H1, H2**, and other heading tags to structure your content properly. Header tags improve both readability and SEO ranking.

### 4. On-Page SEO (Technical SEO)

**Title Tags and Meta Descriptions** are crucial for search visibility. SEOptimer shows how your site appears on Google with the correct tags.

**Keyword Consistency:**

- Emphasize important keywords throughout your content
- Ensure keywords appear naturally in your copy

**Content Volume:**

- Avoid scanty pages
- Ensure your site has enough content for SEO effectiveness
