Navigating Framer's pricing plans can be a bit tricky, especially if you're an individual or a small business trying to find the best fit. In this guide, we'll break down each plan in simple terms to help you choose the right one for your needs.IntroductionFramer is a powerful web design tool that offers various pricing plans to cater to different users. Whether you're just starting out or looking to expand your online presence, understanding these plans will help you make an informed decision.Free PlanFeatures:Design and Publishing Tools: Access to Framer's core design features.CMS Collections: Use up to 10 collections to manage dynamic content.Page Limit: Create up to 1,000 pages for your site.Limitations:Custom Domain: Not available; your site will have a Framer subdomain.Branding: A "Made in Framer" banner appears on all pages.Visitor Cap: Limited to 1,000 visitors per month.Ideal For:This plan is great for personal projects, learning, or experimenting without any cost.Mini PlanPrice: $5 per site per month (billed annually).Features:Custom Domain: Connect your own domain.No Framer Branding: The "Made in Framer" banner is removed.Custom Code: Add code to the <head> and <body> sections of your site.Limitations:Page Limit: Only a main page and a 404 error page are allowed.No CMS Collections: Dynamic content features are not included.Visitor Cap: Limited to 1,000 visitors per month.Form Submissions: Up to 50 submissions per month.Ideal For:Perfect for simple landing pages or one-page websites. It's also suitable for testing ideas or showcasing a minimal online presence.Basic PlanPrice: $15 per site per month (billed annually).Features:Page Limit: Support for up to 1,000 pages.CMS Collections: Access to 2 collections for dynamic content.Password Protection: Secure your site with passwords.Increased Resources: More bandwidth and storage.Form Submissions: Up to 500 submissions per month.Ideal For:Suitable for small businesses or personal blogs that need multiple pages and dynamic content. This plan supports up to 10,000 visitors per month.Pro PlanPrice: $30 per site per month (billed annually).Features:Page Limit: Support for up to 10,000 pages.CMS Collections: Access to 10 collections.Staging Environment: Test changes before making them live.Advanced Analytics and Cookie Banner: Gain insights into site traffic and ensure compliance.Form Submissions: Up to 2,500 submissions per month.Ideal For:Designed for growing businesses or content-rich sites with significant traffic. Teams that need collaboration features and advanced site management tools will find this plan beneficial.Business PlansOverview:Tailored for larger organizations with advanced needs, these plans offer higher limits and additional features like increased storage, bandwidth, and enhanced support.Plans:Startup Plan: $75 per site per month (billed annually).Pages: Up to 15,000.CMS Collections: 20 collections.Bandwidth: 200 GB.Scaleup Plan: $200 per site per month (billed annually).Pages: Up to 30,000.CMS Collections: 30 collections.Bandwidth: 500 GB.Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing.Features: Tailored solutions with custom limits, security features, and dedicated support.Ideal For:Established businesses with high traffic and complex site requirements. Teams needing advanced collaboration tools and priority support will benefit from these plans.Additional ConsiderationsLocalization:Add extra languages for $15 per month on personal plans and $40 per language per month on business plans.Upgrading Plans:You can upgrade as your site grows, paying only the difference in price.Form Submissions and Bandwidth:Each plan has specific limits. Ensure your chosen plan matches your expected traffic and interaction levels.ConclusionChoosing the right Framer plan depends on your current needs and future goals. It's often wise to start with a lower-tier plan and upgrade as your requirements evolve.Call to Action:Ready to build your website with Framer? Explore the Framer pricing plans and start your journey today!
